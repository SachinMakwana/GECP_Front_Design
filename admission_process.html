Admission Process Government Engineering College

Government Engineering College, Patan (GECP), is a government aided educational institution located in Katpur, near Patan, Gujarat. The institute offers undergraduate level engineering courses. The college was founded in April 2004 and is affiliated to Gujrat Technological University (GTU).


Admission Process

Only online mode of application is allowed. Candidates can register and apply through GUJCET website.
Registration fees of INR 350 can be paid at the “Help Centre” while purchasing the information booklet and PIN.
10+2 in science stream with minimum 45% marks for B. Tech programs.
Only GUJCET or JEE Main qualified candidates are eligible for B. Tech counselling and admission.
All candidates are selected for admission through GUJCET counselling only.

Government Engineering College, Patan Reservation 2020

After filling all the requisite details, the applicant will then need to upload the scanned copies of photograph and signature in the specified format. The candidate must obtain a DD of INR 300 in favor of "Secretary, Gujarat Common Entrance Test Cell” payable at Gandhinagar. Upon submission of application form, a unique application number will be created for each applicant which he/she will need to preserve for future correspondence. A print-out of the complete application form and self-attested photo copies of the required documents must be submitted at nearby help Centre from June 1 to June 12. The requisite acknowledgement slip after submission of registration form is to be collected from the Help Centre. Candidates who qualify GUJCET will need to attend counselling for further admission process.
